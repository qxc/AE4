When all spawners are defeated, the players win. When a player deals damage to a spawner that is surrounded by creep, reduce that damage by 2, minimum 1. When creep is placed on an icon, resolve that icon's effect. When the board has no empty spaces, Gravehold is overrun and the players lose.};\diffR{};
