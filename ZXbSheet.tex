\begin{tikzpicture}

\name{Sunless Sovereign \image{N5} \hp{70}};
\setup{Place the Sunless Sovereign track next to this. Place a drill on the first space of each track.}
\unleash{Gravehold suffers 2 damage for each diamantium in play. The nemesis gains 1 nemesis token. If the nemesis has 3 or more nemesis tokens and there are fewer than three diamantium on the track, place a diamantium into play and the nemesis loses three nemesis tokens.}
\difficulty{}
\rules{When a player deals damage to the nemesis besides with a drill, prevent that damage. Each drill is a minion with infinite health. When a player deals damage to a drill, if that instance of damage is at least 1/2/4 for the left/middle/right chutes, it moves down 1 space. When a drill moves down off the chute, resolve the text at the end of that chute. When diamantium is placed, place it directly below the drill that's closest to the nemesis. If that drill is on the last space of the track, move that drill up 1 space and place the diamantium where it was. Diamantium is a minion with 5 health. Drills cannot move past or enter a space where diamantium is. There can be at most 1 diamantium per column.};\diffR{5};

\unleash{Draw a card from the threat deck.}
\difficulty{}
\rules{The threat is equal to the sum of the threat cards in play. If the total threat is 10 or higher, the nemesis pounces immediately. \newline Once on any player's turn, that player may scavenge or distract the nemesis, but not both. When a player scavenges, draw a card from the threat deck up to three times. After each draw, if the nemesis didn't pounce, the player may stop scavenging and gain one reward based on the number of cards drawn from the threat deck that turn. When a player distracts the nemesis, that player loses 1 charge to discard a threat card from play. \newline 1: Focus one of their breaches. \newline 2: Gain a card that costs 4 \Money or less from the supply. \newline 3: Gain a Sunshotgun. \newline When the nemesis pounces, discard each threat card in play, shuffle the threat discard pile and deck together and place it facedown to form the threat deck. When the nemesis pounces during a player's turn, that player suffers 5 damage, gains no reward, and cannot draw additional cards from the threat deck that turn. Otherwise, Gravehold suffers 7 damage. \newline When the threat deck is empty, shuffle the threat discard pile and place it facedown to form the threat deck.};\diffR{5};
